UAE President's Cup
History Of The Cup
The UAE President's Cup is a professional football tournament that takes place in the United Arab Emirates. Founded in 1974, the inaugural champion was Al-Ahli, although Al-Sharjah has taken the trophy the most times of any club, prevailing 8 times. The competition takes place between some clubs in the UAE Pro League and some clubs in the UAE First Division League. The winner qualifies for the AFC Champions League.
